Rectangular multivariate intergral

Here is a genereal interval, defined over the range `D = [a, b] xx [c, d]`   `int_a^b int_c^d f(x, y) dy dx = int_a^b (int_c^d f(x, y) dy) dx`  

General `D`

  `D = {\ (x, y): \ \ a <= x <= b; \ \ g_1(x) <= y <= g_2(x) \ ("w.l.o.g.") \ }`   `int_a^b int_(g_1(x))^(g_2(x)) f(x, y) \ dy \ dx`
