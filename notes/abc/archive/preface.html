Valuebetting

The `"ev"` for each bet `"ev"_b` is given by the following formula: `"ev"_b = p_t (o_b + 1) - 1` where `p_t` is the true probability (as given by our AI model), and `o_b` are the odds provided for the given selection. N.B. `p_t = 1/o_t` and `p_b = 1/o_b` where `o_t` are the true odds, and `p_b` is the normalised implied probability from the bookmaker/exchange. Note that since odds are usually marked up by the bookie/exchange, they must be normalised to get the genuine implied probability.
